2 types of devices can be controlled using AsTeRICS Ergo:
  1. Most devices, which are operated with an infrared remote control, can be controlled. Therefore you need to acquire an IrTrans.

    • TV (TODO link TV Wizard)

    If infrared light bulbs are used, you can also control the Ambient Light .
  2. Additionally, you can control devices that are connected to a socket and normally turned on and off with a switch. Therefore you require the FS20-System .
